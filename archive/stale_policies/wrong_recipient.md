# Wrong Recipient

Use this when the customer sent money to the wrong recipient, wrong bank, or wrong account number.

Required checks:
- Locate transaction and beneficiary details.
- Confirm status and recipient bank.
- Create dispute for trace and beneficiary-bank contact.

Resolution:
- Mafita cannot reverse a successful wrong-recipient transfer unilaterally.
- If recipient refuses return, customer may need legal/court order process.
- Escalate for compliance support if amount is high.
